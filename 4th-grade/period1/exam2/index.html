🐾 Vertebrates & Invertebrates ✔ Done ▶

The key difference: vertebrates have a backbone (spine). Invertebrates do NOT.

🦴 Vertebrates

✓ Have a backbone
🐕 Dog 🐈 Cat 🐸 Frog 🐘 Elephant 🦈 Shark 🐍 Snake 🦅 Eagle

🐌 Invertebrates

✗ No backbone
🐌 Snail 🦋 Butterfly 🐙 Octopus 🪼 Jellyfish 🕷️ Spider

Arthropods have exoskeletons and jointed legs. They are divided into 4 groups — pay attention to the number of legs!
