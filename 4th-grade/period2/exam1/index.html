🔤 Spelling: K / CK / C ✔ Done▶

All three spellings make the same /k/ sound — learn when to use each one!

🔵 C

Used at the start or before a, o, u

cake cook insect

🟡 K

Used before e, i, or at end after a consonant

take fork kite

🔴 CK

Used after a short vowel at the end of a word

stick chick neck
🔇 Spelling: Silent E ✔ Done▶

When a word ends in silent e, the vowel before it says its long sound (its name). The E is silent — you don't say it!
